## üîî Notification Best Practices

### Email Folder Organization

Create email filters/folders:
- **RFP - High Priority**: TED, Bund.de alerts
- **RFP - Medium Priority**: Regional platforms
- **RFP - Review Weekly**: Lower-priority platforms

### Response Workflow

1. **Daily** (9-10 AM): Check RFP email folder
2. **Filter**: High relevance (form/handwriting/OCR keywords)
3. **Review**: Deadline, budget, requirements
4. **Qualify**: Match to Formalogix services (99.8% accuracy, hybrid AI+human)
5. **Respond**: Within 2-3 business days for high-priority
6. **Track**: Log in CRM or spreadsheet

### Weekly Review

- Check platforms directly (not just email)
- Review medium/low priority alerts
- Update search keywords based on results
